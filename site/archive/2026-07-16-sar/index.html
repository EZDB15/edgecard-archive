How this card is made & how to verify it

Edge Card v0 is a transparent heuristic, not a trained model: Prime-Power softmax blended 40/60 with the de-vigged morning line, plus a published rules-based pace adjustment. Constants on the methodology page. Structured analysis, not proven edge; tracked publicly either way.

It reads Brisnet figures and pace only — not trainer, jockey, trip, or track bias yet; that arrives with the trained v1 model. Fair odds are 1 ÷ our win probability.
