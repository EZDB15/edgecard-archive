Closing-Line Scoreboard

Did our flagged price hold to post?

Before every race we publish a “play at X or better” price. After the race we grade the final tote odds against it. This is not a fixed-odds CLV claim — on the tote everyone gets the closing price. It records whether the price we flagged survived the late money.

Calibration

When we say 30%, do they win 30%?

Across every runner we priced — not just the flags — binned by our model win probability against how often they actually won. The probabilities were hash-chained before post, so this is an honest reliability check, not a backtest.
